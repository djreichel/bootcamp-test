BIOGRAPHY

D.J. Reichel

(Since sometime in the mid 1980s) I've been an art director/graphic designer working on mostly printed projects for 30+ years. When I first started in this line of work I sat at a drawing board and a few of the tools I used regularly were a t-square, exacto knife and rubber cement. I bought my first computer in 1992. It was a MAC IIci with 8 MB RAM and 80 MB storage. I remember it would take several minutes to open a fairly small Photoshop file. And then it would take several minutes to save the same file. Nobody was ever in a hurry back then. (January 2019) I've become a new student in the CWRU Coding Bootcamp focused on full stack web development.
